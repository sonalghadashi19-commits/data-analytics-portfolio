## Project 1: Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis
**Tech Stack:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Performed EDA on 9,800+ retail records to identify key drivers of sales and profitability.

**Key Insights:**
- High sales doesn't guarantee high profit — Furniture and Central region showed strong sales but low margins
- Tables, Bookcases and Supplies are loss-making sub-categories driven by heavy discounting
- Correlation analysis confirmed Discount-Profit relationship (-0.22)

## Project 3: Customer Churn Analysis & Prediction
**Tech Stack:** Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, SQL, Tableau, Streamlit

End-to-end churn analysis including EDA, SQL queries, ML model and deployed web app.

**Key Insights:**
- Overall churn rate: 26.57%
- Month-to-month customers churn 42.7% vs 2.8% for two-year contracts
- Churned customers have avg tenure of 18 months vs 38 months
- Electronic check payment has highest churn rate (45.3%)
- Logistic Regression model achieved 79% accuracy
